Our Visions

Love Decoded team is a group of students from NIT Calicut.

The vision of the Love Decoded team is to reach out the needy ones in the society and also in the campus. We make frequent visits to orphanages & old age homes and provide the helps that we could do as students. We dream a world where everyone love others as they love themselves.

Our Team

Love one another. As I have loved you ~ Jesus Christ

We are a team having members from all the branches & years of NITC working together for the society without any barriers of language or culture. There are many ones around us those who are not having anyone to care them, to give them food or to love them as the love and care we are getting. The Love Decoded team is involved in spending time which such people and helping them. Anyone who are interested in the social activities can join the team.
