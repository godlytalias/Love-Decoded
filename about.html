LOVE DECODED

Spreading the love of Christ.

Our Visions

Love Decoded team is a group of students in NIT Calicut who want to spread the joy of life to others.

 Love Decoded wants to share that the real reason for joy in Christmas is Jesus Christ to the students and the general community in and around NIT Calicut.

 Our vision is to reach out to the needy ones in the campus and also in the society. We make frequent visits to orphanages & old age homes and provide whatever help that our little hands can do. We dream a world where everyone love others as they love themselves.

Our Team

 Love one another as I have loved you ~ Jesus Christ

 We are a team having members from all branches, years & degrees of NITC working together for the society without any barrier of language or culture. There are many ones around us those who are not having anyone to care them, to give them food or to love them as the love and care we are getting. The Love Decoded team is involved in spending time which such people and helping them. Anyone who are interested in the social activities are welcome to join us.
